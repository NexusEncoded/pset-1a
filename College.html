College Basketball

What is college basketball?

College basketball is basketball played by college students. There are three divisons going from Divison I down to Divsion III. Most people choose to cheer for the college that they have attended or love. College basketball has a wide fan base as it is followed by many people throughout the world. You can read more here.

Information on the 2019-2020 Season

The 2019–20 NCAA Division I men's basketball season will begin on November 5, 2019. the first tournament will be the 2K Sports Classic and the season will end with the Final Four in Atlanta on April 6, 2020. Practices will officially begin in late September. Get more info here.
